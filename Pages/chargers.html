BC 112A & 112C Power-Flex Chargers

The BC 112 series are our best, most full featured chargers. Using our unique Power-Flex process, these chargers lower the internal resistance of your cells, giving more voltage and current flow under heavy loads. It's like running a 1/2 to 1 turn hotter motor, plus cells stay cooler. Power-Flex also erases cell memory, for noticeably longer run times and extends cell life up to three times.
POWER-FLEX applies a specially timed negative discharge pulse to the batteries while charging. This "burps" the cells, to reduce internal pressure, so they take better charge. The circut also improves plate crystal structure and prevents undesirable memory build-up. With the increased active surface area, cells have lowered resistance, and deliver noticeably more acceleration and power, longer.

3000 mAh Rated

All BC112 series chargers produced since 1997 have been designed for use with 3000 mAh Ni-MH cells. If your charger has H27 or higher software, then the charger will do these cells. (The software version of the charger flashes on the screen during power-up.) You should only use the "P" charge mode, not the "P2". The "P" charge mode provides the high sensitivity and high accuracy voltage detection required for the these cells. (.02-.04v cutoff) DO NOT repeak the cells unless they have fully cooled. (P2 mode is less sensitive and can overcharge the cells.) If you have the newest H30 software, then you have 3 charge sensitivity levels from which to choose. (P, P1, P2) Use the "P" mode.

DIS 350 BATTERY DISCHARGER

NEW This device discharges, matches, tests and conditions cells. Use on 1-12 cell packs. Discharge current is settable 1-30 amps to cover all applications. Three discharge modes, LINEAR, PULSE,and STEP. Linear provides conventional constant discharge current. Pulse mode low frequency pulse ramps current up and down during discharge, to simulate acceleration and top speed. Step mode varies the current in a semi-random pattern that simulates driving road courses. Cells get trained to perform. 45 amp stress test weeds out weak cells that pass lesser tests, but fail when put into a car. Portable for track use.
